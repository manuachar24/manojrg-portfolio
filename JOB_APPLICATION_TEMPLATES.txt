💡 PRO TIPS FOR FRESHERS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Personalize every application - Don't send the same email to everyone
2. Highlight projects in your GitHub - Add good READMEs to your repositories
3. Connect on LinkedIn first - Then send message (increases response rate)
4. Mention specific tools they use - Shows you've researched the company
5. Lead with impact metrics - "80% accuracy" > "built a model"
6. Include your portfolio link - Make it easy for them to see your work
7. Follow up politely - After 7-10 days if no response
8. Apply early morning - Monday-Wednesday have better response rates
9. Target 5-10 quality applications/day - Not 50 generic ones
10. Practice interviews - Use Pramp, LeetCode, or mock with peers
